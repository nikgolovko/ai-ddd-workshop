[.lead]
This document provides two things for Step 2 of the VQS DDD Workshop:

. A *blank template* for running an EventStorming session (Section 1).
. A *fully worked example* drawn from the VQS edge-inspection system (Section 2).

Duration: *120 minutes*. +
Goal: Map the full domain lifecycle on a timeline using colour-coded sticky notes. Surface hotspots. Identify the boundaries of emerging bounded contexts.

=== Sticky Note Legend

[cols="2,3,4", options="header"]
|===
| Colour | Type | Rule

| 🟧 Orange | Domain Event | Past tense. A fact that happened. e.g. "DefectDetected"
| 🟦 Blue | Command | Imperative. An intention that causes an event. e.g. "AssignDecision"
| 🟨 Yellow | Aggregate | Noun. The thing that handles commands and emits events. e.g. "InspectionCase"
| 🟩 Green | Policy / Reaction | "Whenever X, then Y." Automation rules and human reactions.
| 🟥 Red | Hotspot | An unresolved question, conflict, or gap. Park and move on.
| 🟪 Purple | External System | A system or actor outside your domain boundary.
|===

=== Phase 4 — Policies and Reactions (Green)

[cols="1,8", options="header"]
|===
| # | Policy

| P01 | Whenever ≥3 OverrideSubmitted events of the same DefectType with model-error ReasonCategory occur within a rolling 24 h window, then RequestRCA
| P02 | Whenever RootCauseSuggested arrives at Case Management, then pass through ACL and CreateRecommendation
| P03 | Whenever DBV review_date passes without renewal, then raise ReviewOverdueAlert to Plant Manager
| P04 | Whenever DriftDetected fires, then alert ML Engineer (owner of retraining decision — RACI unresolved, hotspot H6)

=== Phase 5 — Hotspots (Red)

[cols="1,6,3", options="header"]
|===
| # | Hotspot description | Priority

| H1
| When does an Override pattern trigger an RCA request? The team disagrees: any override vs repeated same-type vs only model-error category.
| High

| H2
| DriftDetected currently has a 72 h lag (weekly report, manual review). No real-time detection exists.
| High

| H3
| DecisionBand thresholds were set at initial deployment 14 months ago. Nobody knows who owns them or when they were last reviewed.
| High

| H4
| Override reason codes do not exist. Free-text justification cannot drive retraining or satisfy EU AI Act Article 10 requirements.
| High (resolve first)

| H5
| RootCauseHypothesis (raw LLM output) has no Anti-Corruption Layer between RCA and Case Management today. Raw JSON is passed directly.
| High

| H6
| Who owns the retraining decision? When DriftDetected fires, no process exists for who evaluates it, approves retraining, and signs off the new model version.
| High

| H7
| Model retraining is entirely manual. No automated pipeline, no version management for edge deployments, no rollback mechanism.
| Medium
|===

=== Phase 6 — External Systems (Purple)

[cols="4,6", options="header"]
|===
| External system | Interaction with domain

| PLC (Programmable Logic Controller)
| Sends panel-arrival signal; receives diverter trigger command. Upstream of Inspection context.

| HMI Workstation (Petra's terminal)
| Displays HMI alert and thumbnail; receives override submission. Interface layer of Inspection context — not a separate bounded context.

| ERP / MES (Manufacturing Execution System)
| Receives quality disposition data (SCRAP / PASS counts) from Case Management. Downstream consumer — Open Host Service boundary.
